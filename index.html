About The Reunion

Where

When

Schedule

  • Thursday, May 21
    • No official class programming for '05. Houses open at 4:00 pm and Smith will provide dinner starting at 5:30 pm.
  • Friday, May 22
    • Morning: College programming - full schedule here
    • 12:00 pm: Lunch
    • Afternoon: College programming - full schedule here
    • 5:30-8:30 pm: Class of 2005 Social Hour and Dinner with the Class of 2015
      Join us for a fun evening networking with the Class of '15 over drinks and dinner.
    • 8:30-11:00 pm: Class of 2005 Hangout at the Northampton Brewery
      We'll be hanging out at the Northampton Brewery for a few hours - come catch up with classmates!
    • 8:30-Whenever: Informal Class of 2005 Movie Night
      We'll have movies, popcorn and chilling at Class HQ if the Brewery isn't your speed.
  • Saturday, May 23
    • 9:15 am: Alumnae Parade!
      It's basically Ivy Day part deux. We'll be marching as a Class with fun signs. Wear white! Followed by the annual Alumnae Association meeting.
    • 12:00 pm: Picnic Lunch for all Reunion Classes
    • Afternoon: College programming - full schedule here
    • 3:00-4:00 pm: Tea for the Class of 2005
      We'll gather in our Class HQ for an old-school tea, complete with pastries and snacks.
    • 4:00-5:00 pm: Class of 2005 Class Meeting
      Just after tea, we'll hold our Class Meeting to review fundraising totals, elect new service positions, and talk about our upcoming 20th (!) reunion.
    • 5:30-8:30 pm: Class of 2005 Social Hour and Dinner with the Class of 2015
    • 8:30 pm: Illumination Night
      It's just as beautiful and nostalgic as you remember.
    • 9:30 pm: Dance Party!
