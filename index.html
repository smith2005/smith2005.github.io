About The Reunion

This reunion is meant for EVERYONE in our class. We want you to come alone, with friends, with partners, and/or with kids. Most of all, we hope you will come to reunion just as you are, today. No need to wait until you’ve published a book, paid off your undergrad loan, discovered a cure to a disease, been elected to congress, or met the partner of your dreams and birthed a baby into a pond of koi fish, etc.

Where

Smith College, Northampton, MA

When

May 14-17 2020
